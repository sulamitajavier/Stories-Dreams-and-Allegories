STORIES, DREAMS,
AND ALLEGORIES

“Stories, Dreams, And Allegories” written and published by Olive Schreinerin in 1923,
was found 96 years later as an altered book from its previous owner through cutouts of
other poems and hand-written notes. The physical book in itself is now impossible to read,
but the statement of this informal editor remains. Therefore, this publication intends for every
viewer to become an editor. As part of the public domain, the meaning of this book must be
shaped by the public as well. Every chapter has non-changeable images that represent the
author’s piece. Hopefully, inspired by these permanent images of each chapter,
you can reshape and give a new perspective over the Schreinerin's work.
